## What is Ollama?
Ollama is a platform that lets you run large language models (LLMs) locally on your computer, without relying on the cloud. It provides privacy, speed, and control by keeping data on your device. Essentially, it makes advanced AI models like GPT or Claude accessible directly on personal machines.

## RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)

## ChromaDB
ChromaDB is an open-source vector database for efficient storage and retrieval of embeddings.
